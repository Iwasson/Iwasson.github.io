Temtem Twitch Bot

github.com/Iwasson/TemTem-Bot

This is a Twitch Bot that interfaced with the Temtem API. A Twitch bot that I created for a friend who was streaming Temtem at the time, this bot allows any user in chat to query information about the workings of the game. Created with the intention to increase chat participation and improve the quality of life for my friends stream.

Discord Monopoly

github.com/Iwasson/MonopolyBot

What is better than Discord with your friends? Playing Monopoly IN Discord with your friends! We built this application from the ground up to allow up to four people to play Monopoly in Discord chat. Everything was built from the ground up, official rules only!
